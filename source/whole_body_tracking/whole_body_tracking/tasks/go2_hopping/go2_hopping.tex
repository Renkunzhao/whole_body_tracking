\section{Minimal In-Place Hopping Reward}

The current manager-based Go2 hopping task uses a minimal phase-conditioned
reward. Let the episode phase be
\begin{equation}
    \phi_t = \operatorname{mod}\left(\frac{k_t \Delta t}{T}, 1\right),
\end{equation}
where $k_t$ is the per-environment episode step, $\Delta t$ is the environment
step time, and the default cycle time is
\begin{equation}
    T = 1.0\ \mathrm{s}.
\end{equation}
The policy and critic observe the phase through
\begin{equation}
    \bm{o}^{\mathrm{phase}}_t =
    \begin{bmatrix}
        \sin(2\pi \phi_t) & \cos(2\pi \phi_t)
    \end{bmatrix}.
\end{equation}

\subsection{Phase Contact Reward}

The actual contact state is
\begin{equation}
    c_{i,t} =
    \mathbb{I}\left[
        \max_{\tau \in \mathcal{H}}
        \left\lVert \bm{f}_{i,t-\tau} \right\rVert_2
        > f_{\mathrm{th}}
    \right],
    \qquad
    f_{\mathrm{th}} = 5.0\ \mathrm{N}.
\end{equation}
where $c_{i,t}$ denote whether foot $i$ is in contact, $\bm{f}_{i,t}$ be the net contact force on foot $i$ at time $t$, $\mathcal{H}$ is the history window.

The desired contact state is all-feet stance for the first part of the cycle
and all-feet flight afterward:
\begin{equation}
    d_t = \mathbb{I}\left[\phi_t < \rho_{\mathrm{stance}}\right],
    \qquad
    \rho_{\mathrm{stance}} = 0.45.
\end{equation}
The contact reward requires all feet to match the desired phase together:
\begin{equation}
    r^{\mathrm{contact}}_t =
    d_t \prod_{i \in \mathcal{F}} c_{i,t}
    + (1 - d_t) \prod_{i \in \mathcal{F}} (1 - c_{i,t}).
\end{equation}
where $\mathcal{F}$ is the set of feet. Thus the reward is one only when all
feet are in contact during stance, or all feet are in the air during flight.
